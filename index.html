Distance Based Fare Structure Mapper

This visualization displays how the fare paid when traveling between different stations on the EXPO line would change if a distance-based fare structure is implemented when compared to the current 3-zone structure. Station-to-station travel represented in red shows individuals who will pay less than they currently do.

In this demonstration, calculations are based on sample prices of $0.30 per kilometre travelled on the EXPO line. Base prices use the standard 1-,2-, and 3-zone fares of $2.75, $4.00, and $5.50. Differences in price paid will vary when off-peak concession prices are applied.
